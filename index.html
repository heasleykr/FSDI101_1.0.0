Biography

Katelynn Heasley was born in Guam, USA and grew up in Southern California and Wasilla, Alaksa. She began music at a young age and decided to pursue a professional career as a classical percussionist. She attended Interlochen Arts Academy for her high school training and then received her Bachelors from the Univeristy of Michigan. From there, she joined the United States Marine Corps and marched in the Marine Band in San Diego, California.

After leaving active duty, she returned to collegiate studies to pursue a second bachelors in computer science. She is currently taking classes at San Diego Miramar College in addition to attending a Full-Stack Development program at San Diego Global University. After completing her studies, she hopes to enter the tech community as a Junior Developer.
